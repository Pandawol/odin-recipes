Black Forest Cake Recipe

Home

Equipment

  • 9 inch springform pan
  • non-stick cooking spray
  • medium mixing bowl
  • whisk
  • rubber spatula
  • Measuring cups and spoons

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, (250g)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar, (200g)
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder, (60g)
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 cup whole milk, at room temperature
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup confectioner's sugar
  • 1 cup cherry preserves

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 320°F (160°C). Spray the springform pan with non-stick cooking spray.
  2. mix the dry ingredients in a medium bowl.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients in a separate bowl.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined.
    Pour into the springform pan and bake for 45-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted
    into the center comes out clean.
  5. Let cool completely. Then, whip the heavy cream with confectioner's sugar until stiff peaks form.
  6. Place the cake on a serving plate and spread the whipped cream evenly over the top, and drizzle the
    cherry preserves over the cream.
